About Us

We're a Bristol-based planetarium, and we love space! We have designed this website for all space lovers out there!, offering educational entertainment of our vast galaxy for the public and students. This website is a part of our mission to make space education accessible and engaging for everyone. We hope you enjoy exploring the wonders of the Milky Way with us!
